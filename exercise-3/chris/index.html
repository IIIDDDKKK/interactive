Christopher Rice is a New York-based Urban Planner. He completed his MSc in City & Regional Planning at the Pratt Institute in Brooklyn, New York. He is interested in social inclusion, climate resiliency and working to create equitable communities. Christopher is originally from Ohio, where he completed his undergraduate degree in Environmental Studies at Oberlin College. His other interests include film photography and music.
